# WHO Mental Health Data Visualizations

This folder should contain official WHO-sourced mental health data visualizations.

## Required Images

Please add the following images to this folder:

1. **global_conditions_bar.png** - Bar chart showing global mental health conditions (Depression, Anxiety, Bipolar, Schizophrenia, PTSD) in millions
   - Source: WHO Global Health Estimates 2024
   - Format: PNG/JPG
   - Recommended size: 800x600px or higher

2. **onset_by_age_pie.png** - Pie chart showing mental health onset by age group (0-14, 15-24, 25-44, 45-64, 65+)
   - Source: WHO World Mental Health Report 2022
   - Format: PNG/JPG
   - Recommended size: 800x600px or higher

3. **treatment_gap_by_income.png** - Bar chart showing treatment access by income level (High/Middle/Low income countries)
   - Source: WHO Mental Health Atlas 2020
   - Format: PNG/JPG
   - Recommended size: 800x600px or higher

## Where to Find Official WHO Visualizations

- WHO Global Health Observatory: https://www.who.int/data/gho
- WHO Mental Health Atlas: https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240036703
- WHO World Mental Health Report: https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240049338

## Fallback Behavior

If images are not found, the website will automatically fall back to interactive Recharts visualizations using the same data.
